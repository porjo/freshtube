FreshTube

Use a YouTube API key from the Google Developer's Console.

Paste either YouTube URLs containing a username or channel ID (one per line), or just the username or channel ID on its own.

highlight new videos (since last refresh)
hide videos older than days
hide videos shorter than minutes
Used to override video click. Use macro '%v' to insert video URL into target URL (URL encoded).

Note: the API key & other data entered will be saved in your browser's local storage and may be visible to other users of this computer.
